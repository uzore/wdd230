Abuja Chamber of Commerce:

Discover the heart of Nigeria's business landscape with the Abuja Chamber of Commerce. For over three decades, we have been fostering economic growth, facilitating trade, and supporting entrepreneurship in the vibrant capital city. Our commitment to driving innovation and connecting businesses makes us a pivotal force in Abuja's commercial arena.


The Jabi Lake Mall

Jabi Lake Mall is a prominent commercial and leisure destination nestled in Abuja, Nigeria. Boasting a contemporary design and a diverse array of retail outlets, dining options, and entertainment venues, it is a hub of commerce and recreation. This mall serves as a vibrant marketplace, drawing locals and tourists alike, contributing to the economic dynamism of the region. With its modern infrastructure and role as a social and economic center, Jabi Lake Mall enriches the lifestyle of Abuja's residents and visitors.

The National Stadium

The Abuja National Stadium stands as a symbol of Nigeria's sporting and cultural heritage. Located in the heart of the capital city, Abuja, this iconic facility has hosted numerous national and international events, showcasing the nation's athletic prowess. Its world-class infrastructure, encompassing modern sports facilities and vast seating capacity, makes it a hub for sporting excellence and entertainment. The stadium's significance extends beyond sports, playing a vital role in the cultural life of Nigeria and contributing to the vibrant economic landscape of the region.


The Abuja Centenary City

The Abuja Centenary City was a real estate development project in Nigeria's Federal Capital Territory, created to commemorate the nation's centenary in 2014. Positioned near Abuja, the capital city, the project aimed to establish a world-class city for business, commerce, and tourism. It faced challenges related to land acquisition, funding, and development pace, which raised concerns about its progress and viability.


Advocacy for Business Growth:

At the Abuja Chamber of Commerce, we take pride in our advocacy efforts. We work tirelessly to create a conducive business environment by engaging with government and regulatory bodies. Our mission is to champion the interests of businesses, influence policies, and ensure a prosperous future for entrepreneurs in the region.
