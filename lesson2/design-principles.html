PARC: Contrast:

Contrast is part of the PARC principles of used in web design
to create harmony and consistency the color,texture, shapes or sizes of elements on a webpage.
it also shows the uniqueness of two or more design elements.
The Spotify webpage is an example of a webpage that applied
contrast well as it contrasts the uniqueness a dark background, white and light color like blue.






Netflix

Visual Hierarchy

visual Hierarchy is the arrangement of design elements in the order of their important, to guide the user's attention and create a clear and logical structure.
Netflix webpage is one of the webpages that perfectly applied this principle in their web design.
The page has a large and prominent banner image that showcases the featured content, followed by smaller thumbnails of different categories and genres.
The page also uses color, contrast, typography and spacing to create a visual hierarchy that helps the user navigate and explore the content.






Google

Hick's Law

Hick's Law states that the time it takes for s user to make a decision increases the number of choices available.
Therefore, reducing the number of options can improve the user experience and increase conversations.
Google webpage follows Hick's law with its simplicity. The page is simple and cleanly designed with just one user input
for the user to enter their query, the page also has options like voice search, images, and apps that are placed simply.
